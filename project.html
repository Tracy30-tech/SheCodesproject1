Reille Novel Hub is a very special platform where you can read the translated versions of world famous Japanese, Chinese and Korean Reille novels in English. Every new chapters published by the author is updated instantly on the Reille Novel Hub and notification service is provided to the readers.

The aim of the platform is to bring together the genres of Reille novel and web novel and allow you to read and follow them through a single source.

Start reading now to explore this mysterious fantasy world.

In the meantime, there are many features that will maximize your reading quality.

  • With the member system, you can add the novel you follow to your library and see your progress.
  • You can mark the novels you have added to your library as favorites and make a separate list.
  • You can follow your reading history and see which part of the novel you are in.
  • You will be automatically notified when a new chapter is added for one of the novels you have added to your library.
  • You can join the community and discuss with your comments within the novel and on each chapter page.
  • You can like and dislike other users' comments in the comment system.
